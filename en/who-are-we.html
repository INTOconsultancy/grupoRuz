Who are we?

We are the leading Mexican company in the development, design and distribution of products from the best brands.

Grupo Ruz is integrated by the following companies: Operadora Ruz, Fantasy Ruz and Ruz Imagine, among others.

Our main product categories are fashion accessories, bags & backpacks, plush toys, role play toys and seasonal décor; among many others.

We have the best brand portfolio in the market, and 25 years of experience.

What do we do?

We transform common products into fun products. We develop differentiated products for every market niche.

Operadora Ruz

The Company founded in 1991 focused on the design, trade and distribution of back to school accessories, and travel luggage with the most recognized brands in the market.

We currently have a great team of Mexican designers who take the task of developing products from its very beginning, using the best materials and their creativity to offer an attractive product for kids.

Fantasy Ruz

A Mexican Company founded in 2000, with experience in the textile industry, with the objective to design and fabricate costumes of the most recognized characters, always keeping the Mexican market’s needs clear.

Thanks to the innovative designs and efficiency in its processes, the company saw the need to create a bigger and more modern plant, which is currently located in Jilotepec, Edo. De México.

This is where all the manufacture and distribution takes place. We are working on venturing to other countries.

Ruz Imagine

The division focused on the design, trade and distribution of the Toys and Plush category with the best licenses in the market. Thanks to the support of Grupo Ruz, this company now is present at a national and international level.

All of the materials and manufacturing processes are done with the most advanced technology in the industry, which makes our products original, and comply with the highest quality standards and the trust of our consumers.
